Employer Branding

The Employment Value Proposition (EVP) is the collection of key attributes that drive brand perception among employees and candidates. The brand can help you attract and retain talent, or it can drive talent to competitors. Recognizing the value of the employment brand, many companies are taking a disciplined approach to defining the EVP. Today, the EVP can be treated as a company asset that can be measured, managed and improved.

Futurestep can help you take control of your EVP, leveraging proven principles and best practices to understand your audience, align your brand with the needs and values of your audience, and track the impact of your branding efforts as they evolve. With an EVP strategy in place, your organization can realize positive impact across all facets of talent management.
